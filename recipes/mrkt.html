Marshmallow Rice Krispie Treats

Description

These squidgy crispy rice treats are made with just 3 ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 5 cups crispy rice cereal

How to make the Marshmallow Rice Krispie Treats:

  1. Step 1: Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat.
  2. Step 2: Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  3. Step 3: Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.
  4. Step 4: Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13-inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper.
  5. Step 5: Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
  6. Step 6: Enjoy!
